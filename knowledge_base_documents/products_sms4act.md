# Integrated SMS text messaging with SMS4Act!

Send and record text messages directly from Act!




### Extend Your Reach

With open rates more than 4x higher than email, text messaging is an easy and cost-effective way for businesses large and small to keep in contact and stay top-of-mind with customers and prospects.

With SMS4Act!, you can quickly and easily send text messages directly from within Act! and automatically record replies for follow-up and reference.

**Key features & benefits:**

- Easy-to-use text message editor
- Send text messages to individuals or groups
- Automatically record sent messages and replies



### Personalize for Maximum Effect

Increase customer engagement and deliver exceptional experiences by tailoring text messages to specific audiences.

With SMS4Act!, you can create text messages for any occasion, including scheduling, order updates, shipping status, event reminders, promotion notifications, and more.

**Key features & benefits:**

- Use the text editor to manually tailor messages to specific audiences
- Create templates with unique contact and activity information
- Automate unique business or industry best practices to stand out


### Rely on a Global Leader

Text messaging is one of the most widely used communication methods in the world, adopted and embraced by people of all ages in virtually every industry.

With SMS4Act!, Act! users can rely on the power and global reach of Message Media, a market-leading global messaging provider.

**Key features & benefits**

- Send text messages globally
- Manage opt-outs & deliverability
- Powered by Message Media
